Visual Artist

With a Bachelor’s degree in Visual Art and over 15 years of experience, I am a versatile artist with a deep proficiency in art fundamentals like drawing, illustration and sculpture. Also I have profound expertise in CG art, design, and photography.

Growing up in a family deeply immersed in diverse fine arts-ranging from painting to sculpture, fashion, and media arts. I developed a rich foundation in creativity and imagination. This heritage has been instrumental in shaping my artistic vision and executing my ideas effectively.

Throughout my career, whether working as a freelancer or as a senior member of various teams, I have refined my skills in problem-solving, communication, and leadership, adeptly navigating challenges and unexpected scenarios. My involvement in a diverse range of projects, including design, CG art, and more, has continually inspired me to expand my artistic and technical abilities. Leading teams has enabled me to enhance motivation and achieve successful project outcomes. Additionally, my experiences with immigration have granted me valuable insights into adapting to new cultures and environments, further strengthening my ability to connect with diverse audiences and collaborators.

I am passionate about exploring new artistic avenues and contributing to innovative projects, leveraging my extensive background and adaptability to bring fresh perspectives and excellence to every endeavor.

A curated selection of works that reflect a blend of creativity, innovation, and craftsmanship. Each piece tells a unique story of design, bringing ideas to life through meticulous attention to detail.
